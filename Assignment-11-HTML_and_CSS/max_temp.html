Max Temperature

This analysis shows the correlation between latitude and max temperature, measured in Fahrenheit.

From the plot, it is evident that cities located at or near the equator (latitude 0°) experience the highest temperatures relative to cities farther away from the equator. Cities that are south of the equator generally experience warmer weather as well when compared to equidistant cities in the north. Cities 40° SOUTH of the equator are warmer than cities 40° NORTH of the equator.
